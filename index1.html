Who We Are??

Nascent Education Foundation (referred as NEF) is an educational consultancy established with the motive of assisting students from South East Asian countries like Nepal, Bangladesh, Bhutan, Pakistan, Srilanka etc for further studies on their desired destination, It has been working forward to place students on the courses of their choices providing them various alternatives abroad on the various courses like MBBS, BDS, BSc Nursing, Optometry, Engineering, Physiotheraphy, MPH and BPH and lot other courses on full funding basis.NEF also focuses on providing the students with the information regarding the opportunities of scholarships, grants, trainings & more. NEF firmly believes on cordial poise between the students, colleges & stakeholders for the overall development of the educational sector (National/ International) ultimately for each to flourish and thence xecutes its purposeful and thence, executes its purposeful operation via multiple methodologies inclusive of Events, Campaigns, Networks (Socio/Commercial), Technology etc.

Nascent education foundation has given its best for the students desire destination. It has always considered students comfort zone.
